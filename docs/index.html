About

Life on Land, or Sustainable Development Goal 15 (SDG 15), focuses on the protection, restoration, and sustainable use of terrestrial ecosystems. Our planet’s forests, mountains, drylands, and biodiversity are under increasing pressure due to deforestation, desertification, illegal wildlife trade, and climate change. These ecosystems are essential for human survival and well-being. They purify the air, regulate the climate, provide food and water, and support the livelihoods of millions of people. By taking action today, we can halt biodiversity loss, promote sustainable land use, and ensure future generations inherit a healthy and vibrant planet.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire communities to sustainably manage forests, protect threatened species, and restore degraded land through education, action, and innovation.

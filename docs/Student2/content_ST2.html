Introduction

Endangered species are animals or plants at serious risk of extinction due to threats like habitat loss, climate change, poaching, and pollution...

Major Threats to Endangered Species

Endangered species face many threats, most of which are caused by human activities...

  • Habitat Loss: Forests, wetlands, and coral reefs are destroyed...
  • Climate Change: Rising temperatures, changing rainfall...
  • Illegal Poaching and Wildlife Trade: Animals like elephants...
  • Pollution: Plastics, oil spills, and chemicals harm both land and marine species...
  • Invasive Species: Non-native species introduced by humans...

Understanding these threats is the first step toward protecting endangered species...

Conservation Success Stories

Although many species are in danger, conservation efforts have shown that change is possible.

  • Giant Panda: Once endangered, their population has grown...
  • Bald Eagle: Nearly extinct in the 1970s due to pesticides...
  • Sea Turtles: On certain beaches, their numbers have increased...
  • Bengal Tiger: In India, tiger populations have risen...

These stories prove that with the right strategies, species can recover...
